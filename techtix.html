What makes Espektro?

The kingdom Espektro is divided into two territories: Exotica - the Cultural Fest and Techtix - The Techno-Management Fest. The gala spans over four days in which the aspiring Engineers feed their technological appetite through events in robotics, science exhibition, hackathon and every other innovative technological bout a creative mind may think of. Besides, the geeks and the brainy wizards in games of chess, quizzing, debate, spell-bee, treasure hunt and a boundless gazette of brain stormer. Exotica, on the other hand, is the house to artistic talents, a haven for creative compositions. Starting right from T-shirt painting, face painting to the royal rumble of college bands, the reckless dance mob and to top them all, the guest performances.
